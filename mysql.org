* 相关问题处理方式
** mysql连接数满
   通过gdb动态调整连接数，然后连进去后处理
   #+begin_src sh
   gdb -p ${pid} -ex "set max_connections = 10000" -batch
   #+end_src
** 授权
   #+begin_src sql
   GRANT create,alter,select,update,insert,delete ON *.* TO user@"%.%.%.%" IDENTIFIED BY 'passwd';
   GRANT REPLICATION SLAVE ON *.* TO rep@%.%.%.% IDENTIFIED BY 'rep';
   flush privileges;
   #+end_src
